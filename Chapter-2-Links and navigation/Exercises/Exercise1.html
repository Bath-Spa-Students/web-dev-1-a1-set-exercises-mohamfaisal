Overview

IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma fearlessly embraces the idea of restarting his academic journey and reclaiming his destiny at a place where millions of students attempt the world's toughest competitive exam: UPSC.

Cast

The cast of 12th FAIL includes:

  • Vikrant Massey as Manoj Kumar Sharma
  • Medha Shankar as Shraddha Joshi
  • Anant V Joshi as Pritam Pandey
  • Anshumaan Pushkar as Gauri Bhaiya
  • Priyanshu Chatterjee as DSP Dushyant Singh
  • Geeta Aggarwal Sharma as Pushpa Sharma, Manoj's mother
  • Harish Khanna as Ramveer Sharma, Manoj's father
  • Sarita Joshi as Manoj's grandmother
  • Sanjay Bishnoi as Naval
  • Vikas Divyakirti as Himself
  • Vijay Kumar Dogra as Sundar
  • Perry Chhabra as Rajini Sharma, Manoj's younger sister
  • Neeraj Kalra as Professor Prabhakar Joshi, Shraddha's father
  • Darius Chinoy as Mr. Solanki
  • Radhika Joshi as Tanya
  • Fasi Khan as Kishan, Shraddha's house servant
  • Rahul Dev Shetty as Mr. Mehta, Manoj's UPSC interviewer
